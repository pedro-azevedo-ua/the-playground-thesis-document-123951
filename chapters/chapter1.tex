\chapter{Introduction}%
\label{chapter:introduction}

\begin{introduction}

While the digital transformation had a big impact on how individuals track their fitness and individual sports, like running or cycling, there is still a gap in team sports.
In Lisbon there are several public basketball courts where anyone can go and play, either you go with friends or alone, you have been playing since you were sixteen years old or you are thirty and you just started, you go to play casual games or just shoot around.
This looks perfect, until you start talking with different players and their experience in this public courts and playing with other players.
Some complain about the conditions, others say that nobody is there when they go, while some say it is always full of people and other do not like how other players behave on the court.
This is where the project idea started to form, a digital mobile platform where amateur players can organize casual games and find the court that best satisfy the player's need.
To really understand this, a survey was made

\end{introduction}
